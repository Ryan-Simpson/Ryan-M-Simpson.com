A premium personal portfolio for Ryan Simpson, a robotics/software engineering student focused on:
- autonomy
- perception
- SLAM
- LiDAR processing
- sensor fusion
- path planning
- embedded systems
- simulation
- digital twins

# Register

brand

# Users

- Robotics recruiters
- Autonomy engineering teams
- Research labs
- Internship recruiters
- Engineering professors
- Technical collaborators

# Primary Goal

Position Ryan as a serious robotics software engineer and autonomy/perception candidate, not a generic CS student.

# Tone

Technical, restrained, premium, research-oriented, intelligent, concise.

# Strategic Principles

- Lead with robotics/autonomy credibility.
- Prioritize clarity over flash.
- Make projects feel concrete and technically impressive.
- Use motion only when it improves comprehension or polish.
- Keep the site recruiter-readable.

- Startup SaaS landing pages
- Neon cyberpunk visuals
- Excessive gradients
- Overuse of glassmorphism
- Random floating blobs
- Identical card grids
- Gradient text
